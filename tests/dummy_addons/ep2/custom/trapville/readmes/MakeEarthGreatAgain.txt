Summary

Shortly after the events of Half-Life 2: Episode 2, Gordon and Alyx must take a detour en route to the Borealis when their helicopter is damaged. While trying to find a long range transmitter, Gordon makes the disturbing discovery that not all former citizens joined the resistance.
